Departments

Computer Science Engineering

This branch of engineering involves the study of computer hardware, software, and their integration to solve complex problems in various fields.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

This engineering branch is focused on the design and development of electronic devices, circuits, communication systems, and their integration with computing systems.

Mechanical Engineering

This field of engineering involves the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems and machines, ranging from small components to large-scale systems.

Civil Engineering

This branch of engineering deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as buildings, roads, bridges, and water supply systems.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

This engineering field is concerned with the design and development of electrical and electronic systems, ranging from small-scale electronic devices to power systems and communication networks.

Chemical Engineering

This branch of engineering deals with the design, development, and operation of processes that involve the transformation of raw materials into useful products, such as fuels, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals.

Faculty

Our faculty members are not only highly qualified and experienced but also come from diverse academic and professional backgrounds. They bring a wealth of knowledge, skills, and perspectives that enrich the learning experience of our students. Additionally, our faculty members are approachable and accessible, and are committed to providing personalized attention and mentorship to every student.

Research

Our research culture is vibrant and dynamic, with a focus on interdisciplinary research that addresses real-world challenges and contributes to the advancement of knowledge. Our faculty members are engaged in cutting-edge research in areas such as artificial intelligence, sustainable energy, and healthcare, among others. We provide our students with numerous opportunities to participate in research projects, work alongside faculty members, and gain valuable research skills.

Placement

We have a strong industry connect and have established partnerships with leading companies and organizations in various sectors. Our placement cell works tirelessly to provide our students with excellent placement opportunities in top companies, both nationally and internationally. We offer training and skill development programs, mock interviews, and career guidance to prepare our students for successful careers.
